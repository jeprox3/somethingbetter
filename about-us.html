About Us
What We Do
Our specialty is cleaning, organizing, and de-cluttering homes and businesses • We provide on-going service, as-called service, and one-time service--whatever YOU need. There are no cleaning contracts to sign. Your satisfaction with our services is all we need.
Where We Do It
Cleaning services are offered within 10 mile from our Souderton office. Typical areas of service include: Blooming Glen, Chalfont, Colmar, Dublin, Earlington, Fountainville, Harleysville, Hatfield, Hilltown, Kulpsville, Lansdale, Line Lexington, Montgomeryville, North Wales, Perkasie, Plumsteadville, Quakertown, Sellersville, Silverdale, Souderton, Telford, Trumbauersville, and Tylersport. Organizing services often have us traveling up to 20 miles away from our Souderton office. If you're not sure whether we travel to your area, please don't hesitate to give us a call and ask!
What YOU Need To Do

What We Did Back in 2004 ...
Margaret Zglinicki began Something Better in 2004 because she wanted an opportunity to use her organizing skills to serve others while providing a way to earn income while continuing to homeschool her children. She discovered that there are many people in our area who lack the time, energy, knowledge, or desire to clean and organize and that there is a need for reputable, efficient, professional, and easy-to-work-with assistance. We love being the answer when people cry out, "There's got to be something better!"
